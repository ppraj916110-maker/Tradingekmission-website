Advanced Intraday Trading — Playbook

A compact, actionable playbook: principles, strategies, risk management, psychology and a live intraday chart below.

Core Principles to Master

  • Speed & Precision – Execute trades quickly with minimal hesitation.
  • Risk Management – Risk no more than 1–2% per trade.
  • Capital Preservation – Avoid large losses; focus on consistent gains.
  • Adaptability – Adjust strategies based on market conditions.

Advanced Market Analysis

  • Multi-Timeframe Analysis – Use H1/4H for trend, 1–5m for entries.
  • Indicators – VWAP, EMA(9/21), RSI divergence, Order Flow tools.
  • Price Action – Pin bars, engulfers, breakouts with retests.
  • Volume & Liquidity – Volume Profile & Level-2/DOM for large players.

Example Intraday Workflow

Phase Actions
Pre-Market Identify key levels, check global markets & news, select 2–4 instruments.
Market Open Observe reaction to levels. Trade ORB/breakouts when volume confirms.
Midday Lower volatility — switch to scalping or mean-reversion if edges exist.
Close Prefer strong trends or news-driven trades; avoid chasing.
